A Little About
J&L Real Estate


J&L Real Estate is a leading real estate investor and asset manager based in Birmingham, at the heart of the UK. Working as a team we advise on commercial Property Investment sales and acquisitions both on and off market, Lease Consultancy for Landlords and Tenants, Building Consultancy for refurbishments, dilapidations and project management and Property Management for service charge and rent collection.


Specialising in opportunistic and value-add investment opportunities, we focus on acquiring assets which require our specialist expertise to unlock underlying value and maximise capital growth.

Our investment philosophy is underpinned by:


  • Geographic specialism with expert comprehension of the macro and micro economics affecting our region.
  • Granular defensive portfolio with no singular exposure to one asset class.
  • An expert in-house asset management team.
